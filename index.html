WE FIX CARBON

At Biochar Pamoja, we are revolutionizing biochar production and application with cutting-edge RoCC kiln technology. Our mission is to provide low-cost, sustainable solutions for biochar production, improving soil health, reducing carbon footprints, and creating economic opportunities for communities in Kenya and beyond. Join us in embracing a cleaner, more sustainable way to cook, farm, and sequester carbon.

What is our story?
Our journey began in collaboration with experts in biochar technology. Since 2019, we have been refining the RoCC kiln concept and expanding biochar production across Kenya, with successful implementations in Bungoma and Kisii. Our projects demonstrate the potential of biochar in agriculture and carbon credit markets, ensuring a future where sustainable farming meets economic growth.
Our Process
  1. Fabricate & Deploy RoCC Kilns – We design and install RoCC kilns for efficient biochar production.
  2. Collect Biomass – Various agricultural residues are gathered and prepared for pyrolysis.
  3. Produce & Weigh Biochar – Biochar is processed, recorded, and prepared for application or sale.
  4. Apply Biochar to Soil – Farmers integrate biochar into their fields, enhancing soil structure and fertility.
  5. Track Carbon Sequestration – All biochar production is logged into the CERCS CharTrac blockchain for verification and monetization through carbon credits.
Impact & Sustainability
  • Improving Agricultural Productivity: Our field trials in Bungoma County demonstrate significant yield improvements in maize crops when using biochar.
  • Reducing Carbon Emissions: By converting biomass into biochar, we prevent CO2 release and contribute to climate change mitigation.
  • Supporting Economic Growth: We create job opportunities for kiln operators, biochar producers, and farmers integrating biochar into their agricultural practices.
  • Encouraging Replication: Our model is being explored for expansion into other countries, including Venezuela, Bolivia, and El Salvador.
OUR PRODUCTS
ROCC KILN

A compact, efficient kiln designed for small-scale biochar production. It allows for the conversion of biomass into biochar and energy, reducing dependency on firewood.

TLUD COOKSTOVE

TLUD is a clean and efficient biomass cookstove technology that uses the top-lit updraft process for producing biochar. It involves burning biomass in a controlled, oxygen-limited environment to convert it into clean-burning gases and solid charcoal (biochar).

PANEL KILN

A compact, efficient kiln designed for small-scale biochar production. It allows for the conversion of biomass into biochar and energy, reducing dependency on firewood.
